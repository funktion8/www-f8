Origin and roof

Funktion8 is the home of Organisation as Code.

The idea behind Funktion8 is that many organizations maintain, review, document and improve the same basic processes. Instead of paying for this work again and again in isolation, shared process building blocks should emerge as open artifacts: traceable, versioned, reviewable and ready for concrete industry verticals.

Project family

Verticals emerge when enough shared demand becomes visible.

01

Shared processes

Funktion8 collects recurring tasks, rules, documentation duties and approval paths shared by many small and medium-sized organizations.

02

Open implementation

The shared patterns become open-source building blocks: workflows, policies, templates, review rules and operating documentation.

03

Industry projects

When enough customers, partners and interested people gather around a vertical, a subproject with its own landing page and domain focus can form.

First subproject

NaC is the first concrete proof point.

Notariat8

notariat8.de is the landing page for Notariat as Code: the first vertical for notarial processes, training, maintenance and future collaboration with customers and partners.

Further verticals

Law firms, tax offices, property managers and other industries can follow once sustainable communities of need begin to form.

Shared core

All verticals stay tied to Funktion8: conceptually through Organisation as Code, visually through a shared CI and technically through reusable templates.

Funktion8 Styleguide

One CI for the whole project family.

Tokens

Colors, surfaces, typography, radii and base spacing live in the Funktion8 repository and form the required baseline for new sites.

Templates

Landing pages like notariat8.de should later derive from common layout patterns, components and content rules.

Family link

Each vertical site may have its own character, but it must visibly belong to the Funktion8 family and carry the same quality logic.

In formation

A non-profit structure for maintenance, care and training.

Funktion8 gGmbH

Funktion8 is intended to become a non-profit GmbH under AO 52. The structure is in formation and should protect the open core over time.

Vertical carriers

For subprojects like Notariat8, a non-profit UG can organize maintenance, care, training and partner work around NaC.

Customers and partners

Customers do not fund isolated custom work; they contribute to shared infrastructure that can help several organizations.

Transparency

Open source, traceable changes and clear CI guidance should build trust and make later participation easier.

Next step
